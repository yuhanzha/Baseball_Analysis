MOTIVATION

Baseball is a very popular and traditional sport in the US. The history of US baseball can be traced back to the 19th century. Despite the long history of baseball, it is still the third most popular sport in the US, and in 2017, the major baseball league generated 9.46 billion USD revenue. As big data and machine learning provide us massive predictive power, we want to dig deeper into the hidden patterns and giant data in order to reveal the secret within the world of baseball. This application can:

1 - Player and Team Type Exploration

2 - Player Career Path Prediction

3 - Award Player Prediction

  • Part 1 - Interactive Data Table

    Baseball teams and players exploration - interactive tables to explore players and the teams information

  • Part 2 - Exploration Data Analysis

    Baseball teams and players exploration - using PCA and clustering techniques to investigate the types and skills of players and the teams

  • Part 3 - Career Path Prediction

    Baseball players career path prediction - using 32 years (1985 - 2017) players' salary and player performances data to determine whether the players have reached their peak or not

  • Part 4 - Award Prediction

    Baseball Player Award Prediction - using 80 years (1920 - 2000) of awards and player performances data to predict whether or not a player will receive award, if so, would the player become a potential Hall Of Fame candidate
